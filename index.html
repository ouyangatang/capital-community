ABOUT OUR CAMP

Go Go Girl Code Camp is a day camp for middle-school girls to introduce them to computer science, teach girls to code, and show that it can be a lot of fun!

WHY GET GIRLS TO CODE?


As the importance of coding as a skill continues to increase, with programming as a powerful and applicable tool to mold and utilize our very own surroundings, it seems like only one thing is left behind - girls. Girls have tremendous potential to succeed in computer science and defy the expectations set by stereotypes and industry stigma.
