About us.

Capital Community is a registered 501c3 nonprofit that aims to educate young children, adolescents, and adults on personal finance topics that will positively impact the way the community values money. Capital Community is run by University of Texas at Austin undergraduate students raising awareness of predatory lending in underserved communities. Our organization strives on three distinct pillars: lesson creation, sustainable partnerships, and volunteering. Our people dedicate time to creating informative workshops and lesson plans for young students and adults to provide the community with the information they need. We work with local Austin schools and nonprofits to develop relationships to continue increasing our overall impact through active volunteering in the community.
